Getting Started

Onboarding

Everything you need to know before, during, and after your first day.

📦 Before day 1

  • Sign and return your offer letter
  • Complete IT setup form (link sent via email)
  • Arrange parking or transport
  • Collect your access badge from reception
  • Bring ID for right-to-work check

📬 Key contacts on arrival

  • Your hiring manager
  • HR — for paperwork and benefits
  • IT helpdesk — for logins and equipment
  • Office manager — for desk, keys, access
  • Your buddy / mentor

Support

FAQs

Quick answers to the most common staff questions.

HR
How do I request annual leave? ▼
Submit your request via [HR system name] at least [X] days in advance. Your manager will approve or decline within 2 working days. Add it to the shared team calendar once approved.
When do I get paid? ▼
Salaries are processed on the [X]th of each month. If that falls on a weekend or bank holiday, payment is made on the last working day before.
How do I claim expenses? ▼
Submit receipts via [expense system] within 30 days of purchase. Expenses over £[X] require pre-approval from your manager before purchase.
IT
I can't log in — who do I contact? ▼
Contact IT helpdesk at it@company.com or message @it-support on Slack. For urgent issues outside working hours, call the IT emergency line: [number].
How do I get access to a new tool or system? ▼
Raise an IT access request via [system]. Your manager will need to approve it first. Standard turnaround is 1–2 working days.
General
Who do I speak to if I have a concern? ▼
Start with your line manager. If that's not appropriate, contact HR directly at hr@company.com. All concerns are treated confidentially.
How do I update my personal details? ▼
Update your details via [HR system] or email HR directly. Keep your emergency contact and bank details current — especially after any life changes.
