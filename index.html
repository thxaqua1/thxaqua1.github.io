Welcome

If you're reading this, congratulations — you've officially been accepted into the SMPFinder staff team. Your role is to maintain quality, professionalism, and trust across the platform. Everything you do represents SMPFinder, so always act with maturity and good judgement.

Onboarding

  • All work is done through the SMPFinder Discord.
  • Staff channels are used for logs, communication, and updates.
  • The subdivision server is where management communicates directly with you.
  • SRA are senior-level staff responsible for major decisions and oversight.

Expectations

General

  • Remain professional at all times
  • Use proper judgement
  • Respect all users
  • Do not abuse permissions

Quota

  • Admin → 18 Punishments / 6 Tickets
  • Senior Mod → 20 / 10
  • Mod → 20 / 12
  • Helper → 20 / 12
  • Junior → 20 / 12

Ticket Systems

Partnerships

  • Check final embed carefully
  • Verify screenshot authenticity
  • Server must have 25+ members
  • Ensure advertisement is valid

Report Tickets

  • Introduce yourself first
  • Run: !reportformat
  • Verify proof + user IDs
  • /rename awaiting process
  • Escalate to admin

General Support

  • Stay professional and patient
  • Most issues involve partnerships
  • Ask higher staff if unsure

Escalation

  • /switchpanel to correct panel
  • Reports → Manager Tickets
  • Appeals → Manager Tickets
  • When unsure → escalate

Moderation

Punishment Ladder

Always follow escalation. Never skip levels unless instructed by management.

Common Offenses

  • Advertising → escalating mutes
  • Toxicity → timed punishments
  • Hate Speech → severe punishment
  • NSFW / Threats → immediate action

Commands

  • /warn
  • /ban
  • /mute
  • /kick
  • /history
  • /case
